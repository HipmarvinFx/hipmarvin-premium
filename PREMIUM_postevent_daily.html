Session Macro Brief — 29 May 2026

The session's dominant driver has shifted: Thursday's core PCE printed +0.2% MoM (3.3% YoY) — softer than the feared 0.3% — delivering partial relief to a market priced for a hawkish Fed hold. That data, combined with the Axios/Reuters report of a tentative 60-day US-Iran ceasefire extension, drove S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 to fresh record highs overnight, pressured WTI toward $87, and pushed Gold back above the $4,500 psychological level after hitting a two-month low on Thursday.

The ceasefire MOU is real but unconfirmed: four Reuters sources confirm a 60-day extension with Hormuz shipping resumption, but Trump has not approved and Iran's Tasnim agency has not confirmed the text is finalised. That ambiguity is keeping DXY anchored at 99.28 rather than breaking lower — the market wants the deal but will not price it fully until Trump tweets approval. GBP/USD is defensively holding 1.3450 ahead of Governor Bailey's speech at 9:20am ET, with the BoE at 3.75% (8-1 hold, April) carrying a cautious wait-and-watch bias.

Primary session watch: Bailey's tone at 9:20am ET (14:20 WAT) is the morning's clearest directional catalyst — a neutral-to-dovish tilt opens the GBP/USD short toward 1.3300; a hawkish surprise squeezes Cable to 1.3520 and invalidates the setup. CAD GDP m/m prints at 1:30pm ET (forecast 0.1% vs prev 0.2%) — a miss triggers USD/CAD long from 1.3800 zone; a beat extends the flush toward 1.3720.

Key signal: JPY net-short positioning among large speculators remains at its most bearish since July 2024, with USD/JPY trading 159.39 — now only 61 pips from the 160.00 level where the Finance Ministry previously intervened and where two rounds of BoJ intervention totalling more than $60 billion were deployed in late April and early May.

Keep USD/JPY longs sized conservatively and maintain hard stops below 158.85. If Trump confirms the Iran MOU today, expect a sharp risk-on rotation: EUR/USD through 1.1700, Gold above 4,527, and USD/JPY retreat toward 158.50 as safe-haven demand unwinds simultaneously — that scenario invalidates multiple setups in a single session.
